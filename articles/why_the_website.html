Why the Website?

6 April 2021

Well... writing lets me think. Alone, in my office, at the keyboard. I love to do it. Every day I write about something. Personal issues, anything I want to learn, relationships, ideas, confrontations, tension. Everything that comes to mind. And therein lies the problem. It comes to mind at a very fast pace, and I write it down quickly. Which is great. I really like to do it. But often is lacks precision and it feels unthoughtful, which is not that great.

Unthoughtful thoughts.

I want higher precision, so I can aim better in life and share whatever I find with the people around me. The Website simply acts as an audience and it forces me into editing what I write. Slowing down the pace.

It does not seem wise to let random people in with your un-edited thoughts. They should be tried out in conversation where the counterpart can help you through the process.

Not that I think anyone will ever read this, that is not the point.

To sharpen the Sword of Light just seems valuable.

Of course there are more reasons, but that is the main one.

It helps me ship things.
It gets me on the hook.
It helps me share.
...
It tests me.
It scares me.

Happy reading!
Martin
