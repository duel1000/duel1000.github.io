Moonlight
03 mar. 2016
Today I began my new project, which is a prototype of a game I might want to make. Hopefully it turns out to be good. It might have a forest troll and a witch. And certainly it will evolve around a humble wood cutter, who is just living in a lilac wood without a trace of fear. Nothing is certain though.
I like beginnings. It is always really easy to see the project moving forward. This morning I had nothing, now I have something. And tomorrow I will even have twice as much! That is a statement I cannot make after a hundred days.
Look at that! What an artstyle. Well, I know it's only purple squares and a white player box, but I am telling you it can move! The witch is next.

It itches...
01 mar. 2016
If you have spent any time meditating, you probably know the feeling of your mind racing around like a wild monkey chasing a banana. And monkeys do chase after bananas. I am kinda at the point where I know what I want to do for the rest of my life, which by first thought is really comforting. It actually feels great. I cannot stop thinking about it. So today my time was mostly spent, figuring out how to do just that, what I want. That's where all the mind-racing comes from. And by the way, if you have not meditated before, try to do it first thing in the morning for just 3 minutes. Breath in deep, enjoy the day. Think about it afterwards. It is priceless. And then remember to really enjoy, what you enjoy. Good luck.
